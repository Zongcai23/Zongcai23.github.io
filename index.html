Biography

Micro-/Nanorobotics × Biomedical Engineering × AI

Zongcai Tan is a fully funded PhD student in Bioengineering at Imperial College London, supervised by Dr. Dandan Zhang. He received his MRes degree with Distinction from Imperial College London and his BEng degree with First Class Honours from Jilin University.

His publications include papers in ICRA, MARSS, Cyborg and Bionic Systems, IJCARS, etc. His selected honours include the National Scholarship, the President Scholarship at Jilin University, Huawei Sholarship and the Amazon PhD Prize runner-up recognition.
